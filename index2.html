How to report a Boss found to the site?

There is a character that is on the North America 1 server, in city/tower 1, the character's name is "Il Rucoy Bosses", you just need to add him to your friends list, and as soon as you find a Boss, just send the found to that character, but be quick, because after I receive a found message, it is no longer possible for me to receive another message for the next 2 minutes.
