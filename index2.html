Rucoy Bosses

How to report a Boss found to the site?

"There is a character on the North America 1 server, in city/tower 1, called 'Il Rucoy Bosses'. You just need to add him to your friends list. As soon as you find a boss, send the 'found' message to that character, but be quick, because once that character receives a 'found' message, he cannot receive another one for the next 2 minutes."
